About Me

I’m Bassel Zain Abedin, an Architect Designer specializing in BIM for residential, commercial, and bridge structures. I have 10 years of experience in architecture design and Building Information Modeling (BIM). I have extensive expertise in designing bridge structures in Canada and creating detailed rebar reinforcing steel 2D and 3D plans. As the owner of a successful freelance service on Fiverr, I offer top-notch architecture and interior design services. My proficiency in AutoCAD and Civil 3D ensures precise and high-quality project outcomes. I'm also experienced in Revit, 3ds Max, Infraworks, Twinmotion, and UE5 for architectural visualizations and rendering. Additionally, I am skilled in using AI tools for rendering and imagery generation, including ChatGPT, Evolve Lab (Veras) for Revit, and Midjourney. I am a Member of the Order of Architects of Quebec (OAQ) as an intern architect.
